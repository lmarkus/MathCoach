I love teaching math and it has always been a passion. What I find most rewarding is when I see my students having an 'aha-moment'

I foster a caring, encouraging environment that helps students comprehend, develop skills, and learn! I am available for online tutoring as well.

What I Offer

Your success is my goal. I work with you to expand on the strengths you already have while helping you work through any struggles and gain the confidence needed to move forward with any Math topics and be successful during the test.

When we first meet, I will identify any gaps you might have because I strongly believe a firm grasp of the basics is necessary to succeed. Math builds upward on itself, and without a strong foundation, it can be difficult and frustrating to move upward to the more challenging topics.

I go step by step until the material is learned and understood and I will repeat how the process is done as needed.

After our first sessions, I will develop a customized solid study plan for you including best-practices, so you can become self-sufficient and an independent learner.

I'd also love to guide you to overcome homework frustration by helping you understand homework assignments, so you feel better prepared to complete the work at home while being self-sufficient and gaining confidence.

If you are willing to make the effort I can help you be very successful.
